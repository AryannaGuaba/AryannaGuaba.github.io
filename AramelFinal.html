The Final by Aryanna Guaba

Aramela

Aramela, a scared princess ventures through the spooky forest in order to get home.
However she quickly becomes paranoid as if someone or something is watching her.
This project was supposed to be a fun little final project instead of anything serious.
I'm not a political person and would've prefered to do something lively.
The feeling i'm trying to convey is a sense of fear or something serious.
